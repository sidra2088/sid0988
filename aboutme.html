    • H E L L O


    I enjoy exploring different creative fields to better understand my capabilities and push my boundaries. This curiosity has helped me build a diverse set of transferable skills, including 2D and 3D illustration, graphic design, and simple animation, using a variety of digital tools and software. I have a formal background in Creativity and Design, having studied at various institutions to develop and refine my craft.

    Through this ongoing journey, I’ve also gained insight into the kinds of environments I work best in. I thrive in calm, focused spaces where I can work independently and creatively, rather than in busy or high-traffic settings. My personal interests—such as gardening, painting, reading quirky and relatable fiction, baking, cooking, fitness, driving, and DIY projects—continue to inspire and inform my creative approach.
